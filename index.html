OpenCRAVAT

Open Custom Ranked Analysis of Variants Toolkit

An open source, scalable decision support framework with an extensive catalog of resources to support cancer driver variant and gene prioritization. Highly customizable, OpenCRAVAT does not expose protected data over the web and scales to very large datasets
